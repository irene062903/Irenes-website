Here are some more facts about Santorini

Santorini - also known as Thera in Greek- is one of the most popular destinations inGreece, located in the southern Aegean Sea. Known for its impressive sunset and scenic streets, the unique gastronomy products and the volcano land, Santorini annually gathers thousands of tourists from all over the world. Fira and Oia are the most visited and luxurious settlements of the island giving you breathtaking views and photogenic landscapes. Τhe island has often been linked to the myth of the ancient city of Atlantis, and its disappearance that has troubled generations of scientists.
